Nagarkot Sunrise Hike

Sunrise over the mountains, memories for a lifetime

About the Hike

Escape the hustle and bustle of Kathmandu with our Nagarkot Sunrise Hike. Nagarkot is famous for its breathtaking sunrise views of the Himalayas, including Mount Everest, Annapurna, Manaslu, and Ganesh Himal. After the sunrise, we enjoy a peaceful hike downhill through charming villages, terraced fields, and pine forests to catch a glimpse of rural Nepali life.

Highlights

  • Spectacular sunrise over 8 Himalayan ranges
  • Easy downhill hike through pine forests
  • Visit the ancient Changunarayan Temple (UNESCO Site)
  • Authentic local lunch in a village
  • Private transport to and from Kathmandu

One Day Itinerary

Early morning pick up from your hotel in Kathmandu. 1.5-hour drive to Nagarkot viewpoint.
Witness the golden sun rising over the snow-capped peaks. Enjoy hot coffee/tea with the view.
Start the 3-4 hour easy downhill hike. We pass through Telkot village and lush local forests.
Explore the Changunarayan Temple. Enjoy a traditional Nepali Thali lunch. Drive back to Kathmandu by 2:00 PM.
