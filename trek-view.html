Itinerary

Arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport. Transfer to hotel. Welcome dinner and briefing.
Early morning scenic flight to Lukla (2,860m). Begin trek to Phakding (2,610m). Hike duration: 3-4 hours.
Trek through pine forests and cross suspension bridges. Reach the gateway to Everest, Namche Bazaar (3,440m). Hike duration: 5-6 hours.
Short hike to Everest View Hotel for panoramic views. Explore the Sherpa Culture Museum.
Trek to Tengboche, Dingboche, Lobuche, Gorakshep, and finally Everest Base Camp. Return via Pheriche and Namche to Lukla. Fly back to Kathmandu.
